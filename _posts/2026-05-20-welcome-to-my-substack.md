I write about what happens when AI meets the real world.

I built my own AI assistant from scratch — not because I'm a developer, but because I wanted to truly understand how these tools work. I bought a used PC with an Intel i3-4150 CPU and 8GB of RAM, installed Ubuntu Server, configured Docker, and deployed local AI models. The gateway crashed seven times in one night. The configuration file got corrupted twice.

Now I write about AI with honesty: no hype, no fear.

What you can expect from this space:

- Real stories of building and using AI on cheap hardware
- Honest discussions about what AI can and cannot do
- Practical insights for anyone who wants to start their own AI journey

This isn't a tech blog. It's a record of someone learning in public.

Welcome aboard.
